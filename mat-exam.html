MAT 2021 Registration

Candidates must check details about MAT 2021 Registration from below:

MAT 2021 application form has been released on 4th January 2021.

Candidates can fill their application form through online mode.

Registration process is based on various procedures including filling of application form, document uploads etc.

Upload photograph in 40 to 150 kb and signature size must be in 10 to 150 kb size.

Candidates can fill their application form till 1st March 2021 for PBT and till 16th February 2021 for CBT.

Take printout of the filled application form and keep it secure for other procedure.

Application Fee.

Candidates can fill their application fee through online mode.

Fee can be paid through debit card/ credit card or net banking.

Application fee is non-refundable and non-transferable.

MAT Syllabus 2022

The MAT syllabus is prepared from the subjects mentioned in the exam pattern of this article as:

  • Language Comprehension
  • Mathematical Skills
  • Data Analysis & Sufficiency
  • Intelligence & Critical Reasoning
  • Indian & Global Environment

Candidates should study these topics to the depth to score good in the exam. The authority does not provide any particular syllabus for the exam.

MAT Score Card 2021

Candidates can view and download their score card after three weeks from the date of test. The score card has also send to the each candidate by post. MAT score card is valid only for the academic year.

If candidate lost the score card, he/she can apply for duplicate score card by requesting to the AIMA authority. Through MAT score card, candidates can apply around 600 management institutions of India.
