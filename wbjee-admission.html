WBJEE 2021 INTRODUCTION

WBJEE 2021 Application Form has been released from 23rd February 2021. Exam Date has been released. The exam will be held on 11th July 2021. WBJEE (West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination) is a state level exam conducted by the WBJEE Board. Through this exam, students get admission into UG programme in the field of Engineering & Technology, Pharmacy and Architecture. Students qualifying this entrance test will be eligible for admission into various courses in Govt. colleges, universities and self financed institutions of West Bengal. Here candidates can check details about the WBJEE 2021 application, eligibility criteria, exam pattern, syllabus, etc.

WBJEE 2021 Application Form

Students can check information aboutWBJEE 2021 Application Form below:


The application form of WBJEE 2021 has been started from 23rd February 2021. Students have to follow various steps included in application process – Registration, filling up of application form, uploading of images (as per format and size), payment of fee and printing of confirmation page. Students must have registered with their valid mobile number & email id for filling the form. Multiple application forms submitted by single candidates will lead to rejection. While filling application details, students must take care of the filled details. In case of incorrect details students can also makecorrection when the authority opens the correction window. Students are advised to check eligibility details before applying for the form. Students must keep the printout of the confirmation page safely for further use.


APPLICATION FEE:

WBJEE Application fee is Rs. 500 + bank service charges for general category. For SC/ ST/ OBC-A/ OBC-B fee will be Rs. 400/-.

Payment Mode: The application fee can be paid thorough online mode. It can be paid using debit card, credit card & net banking.

WBJEE 2021 Eligibility Criteria

Nationality: Students must be an Indian citizen.

Age Limit: Applying candidate’s minimum age limit should be 17 years as on 31st December 2021. Applying student must have born on or before 31st December 2004.

Upper Age Limit>: There is no upper age limit for other courses. However for marine engineering, the upper age limit is 25 years as on 31st December 2021

Qualification Eligibility (For Engineering)::

Qualifying Exam: Candidates must have passed 10+2 level or its equivalent exam recognized by the Central Government or State Government concerned.

Qualifying Percentage & Subjects: In the 12th level, students must have passed with Physics & Mathematics as compulsory subjects and any one from the Chemistry, Biotechnology, Biology, Computer Science & Computer Application securing 45% marks (40% marks for SC, ST, OBC-A, OBC-B, PwD categories) in the qualifying subjects

English: Candidate must have qualified English at 10+2 level with minimum 30% marks.

Qualification Eligibility (For Pharmacy):

Qualifying Exam: Student must have passed 12th level or its equivalent from a recognized board or university.

Qualifying Percentage & Subjects: student must have passed 12th level with Physics & Chemistry as mandatory subjects & any one among – Mathematics, Biotechnology, Biology, Computer Science & Computer Application. Qualifying marks should be 45% marks (General) & 40% marks for reserved candidates.

English Subject: Student must qualify English subject in 10+2 level with 30% marks.

WBJEE 2021 Exam Pattern

Know the of WBJEE 2021 in the below given section:


Exam Mode: The test will be conducted through OMR based (offline mode).

Type of Questions: The exam paper will contain multiple choice questions with four options against each question.

Papers: The test will be divided into two papers, i.e. Paper I – mathematics and Paper II – physics & chemistry.

Shift: The test will be conducted in two shifts – morning shift & afternoon shift.

Answering Mode: Candidates are required to use blue/black ball point pen to answer the questions. Students can check the number of questions and marks in the below given table:

WBJEE 2021 Syllabus

WBJEE Syllabus can be checked by the students online from the website. The syllabus will consists of various topics from 10+2 level from the subjects – Physics, Mathematics & Chemistry subjects. Students can go through below mentioned subjects to know about various important topics for the exam:


Physics: Physical World, Units & dimensions, Measurements, Gravitation, Laws of motion, Viscosity, Electrostatics, Kinetic theory of gases, Magnetic effect of current, Electromagnetic induction & alternating current, etc.

Mathematics: Algebra, Trigonometry, Co-ordinate geometry of three dimensions, Coordinate geometry of two dimensions, Calculus, etc.

Chemistry: Atoms, Atomic Structure, Molecules and Chemical Arithmetic, The Periodic Table and Chemical Families, Chemical Energetics and Chemical Dynamics, Coordination Compounds, Physical Chemistry of Solutions, etc.
