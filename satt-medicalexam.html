About SAAT Entrance Exam 2021

SAAT 2021 Application Form has been released on 5th February 2021. SAAT is a university level exam organized every year by Siksha ‘O’ Anusandhan (SOA) University. This entrance test is held to offer admission into various UG/ PG courses. Candidates will get admission into Agriculture, Engineering, Law, Pharmacy, Management, etc. Qualified candidates will get admission into different programmes in constituent institutions/ schools of the university. The application process has been started from 5th February 2021. In this article we have provided complete details regarding SAAT 2021 application form, exam dates, eligibility criteria, exam pattern, syllabus, preparation tips, etc.

SAAT Exam Date 2021

The SAAT 2021 exam dates are given below. Candidates can follow the schedule to apply in SAAT UG & PG courses.


Events Dates 2021
Starting of Application process 5th February 2021
Last date to apply 31st May 2021
Release of Admit card 1st week of June 2021

Counselling process starts July 2021

SAAT Application Form 2021

Students must check complete SAAT 2021 Application form details in the below given section:


SAAT 2021 Online Application has been released from 5th February 2021. Students before applying for SAAT 2021, must check complete eligibility criteria for the programme. Application form should be completely filled up and then submitted by the candidates. The incomplete applications will get rejected by the authority. At the time of uploading of images, students must keep the soft copy of photo to be uploaded. The file size of the image should be less than 1 MB and format should be JPEG/JPG/PNG/GIF. After submission of form, the acknowledgement of successfully submitted application will be intimated to applicant’s mobile number/ email-id. Candidates can fill their application form till 31st May 2021. Students must take print of the application form for future reference.

SAAT Exam Pattern 2021


Check the SAAT exam pattern 2020 below:


Exam Mode & Date: The exam will be held in offline and online mode.

Number & Type of Questions: 120 MCQs will be there in the paper.

Language: Exam paper will be in English.

Time: Exam will be of 3 hours.

Marks: Paper will be of total 480 marks.

Marking Pattern: For every correct answer, +4 marks will be awarded. 1 mark will be deducted for each wrong response.

SAAT Syllabus :-

The syllabus for SAAT 2021 entrance exam will be separately provided by the University for each Course. The syllabus will be different for each UG & PG courses. For UG courses candidates can study their 11th and 12th class syllabus as per the stream in 12th class. For PG course, the syllabus will be released by the university on their website.
