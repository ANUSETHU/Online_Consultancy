JIMPER MBBS

About JIPMER Entrance Exam 2021

JIPMER 2021 examination will be organized by the Jawaharlal Institute of Postgraduate Medical Education & Research, Puducherry. It is a national level entrance exam through which admission will be offered into undergraduate medical MBBS programme. The admission will be offered in nearly 200 MBBS seats. Candidates will have to qualify the entrance examination by obtaining minimum cut-off marks to be eligible for admission. Candidates will be offered admission in the JIPMER Karaikal, Puducherry campuses or various other affiliated institutions. Here are providing details such as application form, its fee, eligibility criteria, and much more regarding JIPMER 2021.

Eligibility Criteria

Candidates should meet the JIPMER eligibility criteria to appear in the JIPMER entrance exam for MBBS.

Nationality: Indian national & Overseas Citizen of India (OCI) category candidates will be eligible for the examination.

Qualification: Candidates must qualify 12th examination with any recognized board or university to be eligible.

Mandatory Subject: Candidates must have Physics/ Biology, Biotechnology and Chemistry with English as mandatory subject to be eligible for JIPMER 2021.

Percentage: Candidates must score 50% marks in the qualifying examination, 45% for OPH- general category and 40% for all reserved category candidates.

Appearing: Candidates appearing in the 12th level examination will be eligible for the examination.

Age Limit: Candidates must be completed 17 years of age at the time of admission. There will be no upper age limit to apply.

No age relaxation will be for reserved category candidates.

JIPMER MBBS Syllabus

The JIPMER MBBS Syllabus has been prepared from the syllabus as prescribed by the State Board Higher Secondary and CBSE for 11th and 12th class. The authority will also provide the mock test to prepare for the exam. It can be get from the official website. The syllabus is set from the subjects such as Physics, Chemistry, Biology and English Language and Comprehension and Logical & Quantitative Reasoning.
