SRMJEEE 2021 Introduction

  • About SRMJEEE: The full form of SRMJEE is SRM Joint Entrance Examination for Engineering, is a national level entrance exam conducted for admissions to B.Tech and M.Tech degree programmes. Every year nearly 1.7 lakh candidates appear of SRMJEEE Through this exam student can apply for SRM group of universities which are SRM Institue of Science & Technology, SRM University AP - Amaravati and SRM university Haryana - Sonepat.

  • Conducting Body: SRMJEEE is conducted by Sri Ramaswamy Institute of Science and Technology formerly known as SRM University, Chennai. SRM University is recognized by University Grants Commission, India.

  • Removal of Negative marking system: In 2017 Negative marking system was removed from SRMJEEE.

  • Attempts: Candidates who are not happy with their performance can apply for second attempt in order to increase their marks or ranking.

  • Offered Courses: Candidates qualifying SRMJEEE 2021 will be eligible for seeking admission to various B.Tech and M.Tech programmes.

  • Colleges Participating: Candidates qualifying in SRMJEEE 2021 will be eligible for admission to the various campuses of SRM University as mentioned below:

  • Kattankulathur
  • Ramapuram
  • Vadapalani
  • Modinagar
  • Ghaziabad campuses of SRM University Chennai
  • Amravati Campus of SRM University Andhra Pradesh
  • Sonepat Campus of SRM University, Haryana

  • Eligibility: Candidate should have passed 10+2 qualifying examination or equivalent from a recognized board of education in India with a minimum of 60% marks in total and a minimum of 65% marks in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics.

  • Attractive Incentives: In order to support and to encourage candidates with exemplary talent candidates, SRM offers direct admission with full scholar ship. SRM also offers various scholarships to other students.

Changes in SRMJEEE 2021

  • Minimum qualifying marks for SRM Ghaziabad (NCR) campus was 55% marks in last year, and this year the Minimum qualifying marks have been reduced to 50% marks.

  • Last year application fee for SRMJEEE was INR 900. This year application fee has been increased to INR 1100.

  • The number of exam cities has been reduced from 150 cities across the country to just 117 cities. This is to decrease the operational costs.

  • Candidates have to scan their Aadhaar Card and upload it during the time of registration to SRMJEEE 2021.

SRMJEEE 2021 Eligibility

  • Minimum Qualification: Candidates who have passed Higher Secondary Examination (10+2 pattern) or appearing in the current academic year with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/ Biology/ Biotechnology as major subjects in a full-time stream from any state board within India, CBSE, ISCE, Matriculation, or NIOS.

  • Age Criteria: Candidate must have completed age of 16 years as on 31st july of admission year. The

  • Nationality

  • Resident Indian, Non-Resident Indian (NRI), holder of PIO or OCI card issued by Government of India are eligible to apply for SRMJEEE (UG).

  • NRIs, holders of PIO or OCI card issued by Government of India who has not taken SRMJEEE (UG) must apply under the International student category only. And such candidates will be given a reduction in fees once they are admitted.

  • Direct Admission: Candidates with exemplary talents will be offered direct admissiion into various courses and will get a full scholarship.

  • Note: Only those Candidates who have passed above qualifying examination in not more than 2 attempts including an appearance for improvement as an attempt will be considered for admission.

SRMJEEE 2021 Exam Pattern

  • Mode: SRMJEEE will be conducted through online process i.e. (Computer-based Test)

  • Questions: Question paper will contain 3 Sections with every 35 questions in a total of 105 Multiple Choice Question's (MCQ's)

  • Marks: Marks given for the answers are +3 for Correct Answers and there is No Negative Marking.

  • Medium: Medium of instruction through out the admission process including SRMJEEE 2021 will be English.

  • Note: Since there is no negative marks, candidates can take advantage of this and attempt all the questions.

SRMJEEE 2021 Application Form

Here candidates can get complete details about SRMJEEE application form:

Application form has been released through online mode. Application through any offline mode is not yet released. Candidates can fill their application form from 12th December 2020. Fill required details including educational, communication, personal etc. Upload the scanned documents i.e. passport size photograph and signature in the prescribed format. Authority will also provide correction facility in last week of March 2021. Application form can be filled till 31st March 2021 for online mode. Candidates must take Printout of the filled application form and keep it secure for other procedure.

Application Fee:

Application fee can be paid through online mode. Application fee is Rs. 1100/- for all category candidates. Candidates can pay their application fee via credit card/ debit card or net banking.
